Mission

AOL ran a ubiquitous software distribution campaign to promote their internet services from approximately 1993 until 2006. Millions of floppy and compact discs were produced during this time and distributed to stores, businesses, and individual residences. The packaging and disc designs paint a vivid picture of 90's and early 00's web design aesthetic. Like it or not, services like AOL, Compuserve, and Prodigy paved the way for modern day I.S.P's; for many of us it was our first foray into the world wide web.

The goal of this project is to catalog as much America Online physical installation software as possible. The packaging for each piece of physical installation media will be included whenever possible. Over time, I hope to include several deep dives into specific files contained within the software and their purposes respectively.
